About the Pomodoro Technique

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method developed by Francesco Cirillo. Work in focused 25-minute intervals (called “pomodoros”), take a 5-minute break between each, and after four sessions, take a longer 15-minute break. This tool lets you customize all durations and tracks your completed sessions. An audio notification plays when each phase ends so you can stay focused without watching the clock.
